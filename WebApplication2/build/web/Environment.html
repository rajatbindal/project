How can we protect the environment?

Supporting nature and thus helping the life on Earth can be done by following different-different easy tasks like -
1)Avoid use of Plastics as much as you can, abandon disposable    plastics, straws, Foams and don't use plastic bags while shopping,    instead use a cotton or Paper bag.

2) Restrict uses of Glass materials and re-cycle them.

3) Recycle paper, thousands of trees are cut to produce paper.

4) Use renewable energy sources like Solar energy, thus reducing     the use of thermal and nuclear energy for your daily needs.

5) Use public transport, despite of travelling in your own vehicles.

6) Use electric vehicles instead of fuel and CNG powered ones.

7) Plant trees, take care of them. You can plant anywhere, on your     terrace, lawn, Garden etc. Become Vegeterian, consume vegs     instead of Non-veg sources.



                                                                                                     
How many new trees should be planted worldwide to mitigate global warming?

It is now known soil contains over 3 times the carbon of all vegetation combined. There is even a 2017 peer-reviewed paper that says the carbon in the top 2 meters of soil is 3,000 gigatonnes, or almost 5x the amount in vegetation.
